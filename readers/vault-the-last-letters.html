Solji decided she herself would try something new, something that no one would expect. Solji visited every entertainment company in search for where she wanted to audition. Her and Jisung had immediately hit it off, and they talked everyday after that. The two of them had hung out every other day when he wasn't busy with his trainee days and Solji wasn't working at her part-time job. Jisung urged her to audition for JYP, and at first, she had planned to so she could be around one of her only friends. However, she ran across YG the day before she was going to audition. Deciding she should go inside and give it a chance, she fell in love with the atmosphere and the artists there. Solji made the decision to audition there, instead, much to Jisung's dismay, but as her best friend he supported her no matter what. She was immediately accepted into YG, much to her surprise. Never did she expect that she'd impress the CEO as highly as she had, but CEO YG loved her. He saw the greater good in her, and he knew that she was going to be big. Days before her debut, she met Jinhwan. He was known as the angel of the new upcoming group iKON, she'd heard much about it as it was the biggest buzz on the media and especially around the company. She didn't know why or what it was, but Jinhwan was immediately head over heels for her. Solji didn't notice it at first, but he was always overly nice to her. He helped her with writing lyrics and choreography, and explained to her the ups and downs of what it would be like to be an idol. When she debuted, he'd watched her from the backstage, cheering her on. She was glad for him, as she never got to see Jisung much and he didn't get to attend because of his busy schedule. But thanks to Jinhwan being in the same company, he was always around, he was always there for her. Perhaps that's why she fell for him, or maybe it was his wits. Solji saw something in him she'd never seen before. It'd taken a while, but he'd swept her off her feet. For once in a long time, she felt happy with him. She had finally found the right thing to conceal her pain, to lock it away in a box, love. Through out the years of her career, Solji became to be known as the highest earning idol in all of South Korea. She was the most successful solo artist the country had ever seen. With all of her hard training and practice, she finally felt like she had made something of herself. She felt like she was useful, with her albums charting on things like Billboard. Not to mention, she had Jinhwan at her side, even though they had to keep their relationship highly private. However, the more famous she grew the more that was demanded of her. She was sent on a world tour, to places she never imagined growing, meeting fans from all over. Barely did Solji get to speak to Jinhwan, but they had still texted every once in a while. But he was busy back in Korea with his own comeback. When she got back, she didn't get to see him either. The paparazzi had stalked her everywhere, so it was a hassle to get to his dorm to even spend time with him. If they even wanted to go on normal things like dates, they had to drive far out to somewhere people hopefully wouldn't know them. Even then, they got caught a few times, but claimed they were just good friends. That didn't mean she didn't get wrath from Jinhwan's fans, worried that he was dating her. The comments had weighed her down more than she expected. Why did people hate the thought of their relationship? was what she always wondered in exasperation, it broke her heart. All she ever wanted to be was someone her parents would look down on and finally be proud of. Solji believed that they watched her from wherever they may be, and as badly as they had treated her growing up, she missed them. They were her only family, and she just wanted to make them proud. She wanted them to be happy they had a daughter, instead of angry that she was even born in the first place. It was why she did everything she did. The hate only got worse until she could barely see Jinhwan anymore. They ended up spending a lot of time at the company, and the only times they were together tended to be in the recording studio writing, or when she was able to sneak to his dorm. Being away from him so often, and with the both of them having comebacks upon them, it raised a lot of stress. She grew depressed all over again as the hate from all sides only got worse. Of course, Solji always had lots of supporters, but like anyone else, she found herself paying more attention to the mean comments rather than the positive ones. With no time on her hands to seek help, her depression only worsened. Through out it all, she found a way to put a smile on her face. She knew that it was her job to make the people around her happy, and it was what she was there to do. So she tried her hardest during her performances, photo shoots, and fan signs. She tried her hardest to be happy. That was, until YG weighed comeback onto her after comeback. She hadn't seen Jinhwan in months, only when they walked past each other in the hall ways and got to say a mere hello. It felt like their relationship was dying thanks to their schedules, but she couldn't complain. Solji knew she couldn't say no to the CEO's commands. So she followed comeback after comeback, losing more weight than she expected and barely eating at all. The sadness in her heart only grew more, and she found herself feeling the same feelings she felt all those years ago. It'd all came back to her like a wildfire, spreading through her veins and corrupting her brain. She remembered it all, she remembered that night with her parents. To think it was the last night she ever saw them was horrific to her, even then. Solji felt like they weren't proud of her, she knew she still wasn't good enough. At least that's what she thought. Like a naive girl, she only ever knew how to focus on the negatives of her life. She should've paid attention to the love she was surrounded by, but the stress and weight of the world on her shoulders was too much. She was too small to bare so much with her weak body. It was why she killed herself on March 25, 2019.
