My First and Last

Play script · High school rom-com, NCT Dream cast

My First and Last

By Kristen Mikeska
Texas Virtual Academy at Hallsville
6/24/2003

Cast of Characters

Choi Yerim: A sixteen-year-old girl with an apparent bright future ahead of her, known for making the highest marks in all of her classes. She’s a clear book nerd, and spends her time either studying, hanging out with her best friends, or dreaming about her inevitable massive crush on Lee Donghyuck.
Huang Renjun: Yerim’s witty best friend who’s been close with her since they were children. He’s very protective of Yerim, and dislikes Donghyuck because of the silly crush he’s had on her since they were young.
Zhong Chenle: Yerim’s other close friend that completes their small clique. He met Yerim when his family moved in next door, and they were close ever since. Like Renjun, he is well aware of her crush on Donghyuck, but isn’t completely opposed to it because he’s the only one who’s friends with him.
Lee Donghyuck: The sarcastic, smart mouthed boy that Yerim swoons over. He doesn’t care much for school and prefers to go out with his friends. Donghyuck is completely clear of the crush Yerim has on him, and he uses it to his own advantage, asking Yerim to do his homework practically every day. He comes off as cold, but on the inside he’s in fact quite the opposite.
Na Jaemin: A straight A student that’s always admired Yerim when she wasn’t looking. He’s always found her pretty, but never did anything because he was scared. Although he is not part of her friend group, the two of them are distant friends.

ACT 1, SCENE 1
(It’s late at night, and the moonlight is peering through the curtains in a dimly lit but brightly decorated bedroom. The walls are a pastel shade of pink, and it’s a spacious room with only a few pieces of furniture such as a bed, desk, and dresser. A petite girl sits on her window seat, his legs to her chest, eyebrows creased downward as she is peering down at her cell phone screen in immense concentration. Suddenly, her phone starts ringing in her hands, and she purses her lips in a thin line as she answers it, placing it against her ear with an all too knowing look on her face.)
YERIM: (sighing deliberately) Yes, Renjun?
RENJUN: (voice blasting through her phone’s speaker, his tone is exasperated and one of worry) Tell me you’re not doing what I think you’re doing.
YERIM: (rolling her eyes as she shifts in her window seat, a look of visible annoyance plastered on her face) I don’t know what you’re talking about.
RENJUN: (growing frustrated from the silly decisions she’d been making recently) Yerim, you’re better than this, I know what you meant when you said that in the group chat!
YERIM: (her tone growing quieter as she thinks about his words) What’s the big deal? There’s nothing wrong with me helping a friend.
RENJUN: I told you, he’s using you because he doesn’t want to do his own work, and everyone in our grade knows you have the highest marks!
YERIM: (frowning, her voice growing dull) Don’t say that, he wouldn’t do that.
RENJUN: He would! I’m telling you, he isn’t the sort of person you should have these sorts of feelings for.
(YERIM grows silent for a long time. She feels broken by just hearing RENJUN accuse her crush of doing these things. Rising from the window seat, she walks to fall onto her bed in exhaustion.)
YERIM: (sadly) I just really like him, Jun.
RENJUN: (exhaling deeply through the phone, trying to hide the shattered tone of his voice) I know you do, Yerim, but I don’t want to see you hurt.
YERIM: Don’t worry about it so much. (she looks to her desk, where piles of papers are spilled all over it, and is reminded of the project she told her crush she would do for him.) I have to get back to work.
RENJUN: (knowing that his words were never going to seem to get through his skull) Okay, I’ll see you at school, Yer.
(Lights dim)

ACT 1. SCENE 2
(The next day at school, YERIM is standing outside her next class with CHENLE. In her arms, she’s holding a large poster-board, with pictures of a Historian influencer on it, along with many facts.)
CHENLE: (looking down at the board in her hands with wide eyes) Whoa, is that your project for history?
(YERIM had been staring blankly at the wall behind CHENLE, in deep thought about her crush like every other day and jumps from the sudden abrupt sound of CHENLE’s voice.)
YERIM: (glances down at the project she’s holding, shaking her head) No, I already turned mine in, Donghyuck asked me to do his.
CHENLE: (frowning in concern, his tone uneasy) He really asked you to do more of his work?
YERIM: (shrugs casually, waving a hand in the air dismissively) Yeah, but I don’t mind, it means I get to talk to him more (she grins giddily) and he seems to have taken an interest in me, even!
CHENLE: (smiles, hiding his nerves) Just don’t work yourself too hard, Yerim.
YERIM: (humming cheerily) Don’t worry about it, Lele (pauses for a second in thought) Say, but you’re friends with Donghyuck, right? You don’t think he’s doing what Renjun keeps saying, right?
CHENLE: (hesitates, looks as if he’s in deep thought before he shakes his head) I don’t think so, Renjun’s just like that.
YERIM: (eyes brightening, she jumps in the air out of joy) I knew it!
CHENLE: (laughs at how happy she is) I’m glad to see you so thrilled, Yer.
YERIM: Thanks, Lele, I’ll see you at lunch.
(YERIM hugs CHENLE, waving goodbye with a large grin on her face. Shifting her grip on the giant poster board in her arms, she enters the classroom.)
ACT 1, SCENE 3
(YERIM enters the class, it’s gray walls are decorated with posters of many figures that were popular in history. Books were on each students’ desks, and most of the kids in the class were sleeping or chatting. Her eyes lock on one person, that sits at the back of the room, focusing on nothing other than his phone.)
YERIM: (slowly approaching DONGHYUCK’S desk, clears her throat awkwardly) Uh, Donghyuck, I did your project. (she sets the poster board on his desk, the sound causing him to look up from his phone.)
DONGHYUCK: (sits up straight in his desk, turning his bored expression into a grin as he meets her eyes) Thanks so much, Yerim! (he immediately goes back on his phone, hurriedly typing something on the screen.)
(YERIM only nods, too nervous to say anything. She turns on her heel and walks back to her desk. Taking her seat, she reads the directions of what lesson they’re doing today and opens her book. All the while, YERIM didn’t notice a familiar student staring at her from the corner of the room throughout the entire class. Finally, the bell lets out a ring, and students rushed out of the room.)
YERIM: (glancing up to see DONGHYUCK walking passed her, she hurriedly speaks up) See you tomorrow, Donghyuck!
DONGHYUCK: (not even glancing back at her) Bye!
(YERIM grins to herself as she gathers her things, throwing her bag over her shoulder before heading towards the door. However, one student was still left alone in the empty classroom. JAEMIN’S eyes trail to DONGHYUCK’S desk, where a certain object laid, and he rose to his feet to walk towards it.)
JAEMIN: (mumbling to himself) Stupid, he forgot his phone. (turning on the screen to reveal his text messages in a group chat, a look of astonishment spreads across JAEMIN’S face.) No way.
(Pulling his own phone out of his jacket pocket, he takes a picture of DONGHYUCK’S lit up phone screen. JAEMIN’S brows crease downward in concentration as he furiously types, and a frown is evident on his face as he hits the send button.)
JAEMIN: (shaking his head as he walks towards the door) She deserves better.
(JAEMIN exits.)
ACT 1, SCENE 4
(The cafeteria is loud, and RENJUN and CHENLE sit at their usual lunch table located in the corner of the large, brightly lit room. They’re both picking at their food, during a serious conversation about what CHENLE spoke about with YERIM before her history class.)
RENJUN: (taking a bite of his salad, speaking through his chewing) So, you told her the same thing I did, right?
CHENLE: (hesitates, breaking the eye contact to look elsewhere) Uh, not exactly.
RENJUN: (coughs on his salad out of surprise, crying in disbelief) What, what’s wrong with you?! You can’t feed more into the lies he’s already got her believing; it’ll only end up hurting her more!
CHENLE: (shaking his head sadly) I know, it’s just she was so happy when she talked about how he seemed interested in her! You never know, Renjun.
RENJUN: (exhales deeply) Fine, if you really think so, ask him yourself since you’re such great friends.
(CHENLE’S about to object against them being ‘great friends,’ when they both see YERIM frantically walking across the cafeteria towards them. Tears are streaming down her face, and she looked like that of a train wreck as she fell into the seat beside them, angrily trying to wipe away the tears that only continued to stream down her face.)
RENJUN: (instantly speaking out of concern) Yerim, what happened? Are you okay?!
YERIM: (taking a piece of fruit off CHENLE’S plate, sniffling) I don’t want to talk about it.
RENJUN: (burning with mixed emotions) I swear, if this has to do with Donghyuck, I’m going to break his nose.
(From the sound of DONGHYUCK’S name, YERIM breaks into more tears. CHENLE exchanges an angry glance with RENJUN, who’s fists were clenched at his sides as he realized. Reaching out an arm to rub her back as she buried her face in the table, he grabs her phone with the other. The two of them peer down at the text she received from an unknown number, that exposed a picture of DONGHYUCK’S messages.)
RENJUN: (reading over the messages, his eyes flaming with fury) I can’t believe he has the audacity to do something like this, just wait until I see his sorry face (he slams his fists on the table) I told you, I told you both!
CHENLE: (looking at RENJUN in disappointment) Is it really the time for ‘I told you’s?
RENJUN: (looks to YERIM, who’s sobbing. His face grows sympathetic, and he shakes his head.) I’m sorry, Yer, I promise that I’m going to give him a taste of his medicine when I see him.
YERIM: (sitting up straight, sniffling and wiping her remaining tears away) It’s okay, at least whoever sent it told me sooner rather than later. (she exhales deeply, shifting in her seat and forcing a smile on her face) I’m over it.
CHENLE: (confused) Really? I thought you liked him a lot.
RENJUN: (slaps the back of CHENLE’S head, smiling to YERIM) It’s good you are, Yer, he didn’t deserve you.
(YERIM half heartedly smiles, and the bell lets out a ring. The three friends rise to their feet, the two boys walking beside her towards the exit doors. As they walk, she makes eye contact with DONGHYUCK, but immediately breaks it. The three exit the stage.)
ACT 1, SCENE 5
(School had just ended, and students were furiously running through the halls, eager to go home. DONGHYUCK is seen walking with his friends down the main hall where all the lockers are located. His eyes are set on one location, where a certain girl is digging through her locker, and he was curious to ask what she was crying about. Just when he’s growing closer, CHENLE steps in front of him, blocking his path to her.)
CHENLE: (face burning with disappointment and frustration) Hyuck, what’s wrong with you?!
DONGHYUCK: (brows furrow in confusion) What?
CHENLE: (rolling his eyes in annoyance, scoffing in disbelief) I saw what you said in your friends group chat about Yerim! Why would you do that to her?!
DONGHYUCK: (eyes widen, astonished) You what, you’re the one who had my lost phone?!
CHENLE: No, but whoever did sent a picture of everything you said to Yerim. (shaking his head disapprovingly) To think I called you a friend. Get yourself together and stay away from Yerim.
(CHENLE turns to leave, not caring what else he may have to say. DONGHYUCK stands there for a moment, his face full of shock as he tries to process everything that was happening.)
DONGHYUCK: (calling out hopefully) Chenle, wait, I can explain!
(CHENLE ignores his calls, and only grabs YERIM’S arm, pulling her towards the exit doors. YERIM and CHENLE exit.)
ACT 2, SCENE 1
(Days later, YERIM is sitting in her usual seat in history class, listening intently to her teacher. After the incident, she’s refused to pay attention to DONGHYUCK. The bell rings, and everyone rushes out of the room except for three students. YERIM rises to her feet, when a new friend she had made walks over to her.)
JAEMIN: (cheerfully) Hey, mind if I walk you to lunch?
YERIM: (chuckling) Sure, isn’t that the regular, why even ask?
JAEMIN: (shrugging, smiling brightly) Trying to be a gentleman.
YERIM: (giggles, playfully punching his arm) You’re pretty good at it.
(YERIM and JAEMIN engage in a friendly conversation, making jokes and laughing. DONGHYUCK angrily clenches his fists at his sides, growing tired of seeing them together all the time, and only narrowed his eyes at the two more when JAEMIN playfully ruffled her hair.)
DONGHYUCK: (mumbling to himself as he rises to his feet and grabs his things) Whatever, what do I care, anyway?
(DONGHYUCK walks towards the door. When he passes JAEMIN, he purposely hits his shoulder with his. JAEMIN only glares at him, but when he tries to move out of his way, DONGHYUCK sticks out his foot and trips him.)
YERIM: (gasps, rushing to JAEMIN’S side and sending daggers to DONGHYUCK) What’s wrong with you, jerk? Go away.
JAEMIN: (scoffing) Clearly a lot, especially after what he did to you.
DONGHYUCK: (dripping with anger, he’s rather speechless once he meets YERIM’S pleading eyes, only spitting down at JAEMIN) Watch your mouth, Jaemin.
(DONGHYUCK exits the room.)
ACT 2, SCENE 2
(A week flies by, and through out it DONGHYUCK had only continued to mess with JAEMIN the closer he got with YERIM. CHENLE, RENJUN, and JAEMIN all had a feeling what was going on, but YERIM was in the dark. YERIM and JAEMIN had just sat at the cafeteria table across from RENJUN and CHENLE, who were in the middle of a conversation so deep they didn’t notice them)
CHENLE: I mean, don’t you think? Why else would he be doing that?
RENJUN: (rolling his eyes) Stop saying that, who cares either way, he’s a piece of trash. No one like that deserves our Yerim.
CHENLE: (shaking his head and mumbling under his breath so only RENJUN hears) But yet you still think you do.
(RENJUN glares at CHENLE, and YERIM and JAEMIN stare at them confused.)
YERIM: (clearing her throat to grab their attention) What were you two talking about?
RENJUN: (picking up his fork and beginning to eat his salad, speaks casually while eating) The scum of the Earth.
YERIM: (wrinkling her nose) Gross!
JAEMIN: (chewing some of the fruit off his tray) Speaking of, Donghyuck really has it out for me recently.
CHENLE & RENJUN: Yeah, we’ve noticed.
JAEMIN: I don’t get what his problem is, it’s not my fault he’s just now realizing how great Yerim is.
YERIM: (smiles at his compliment, but as she thinks about his words it fades) He probably still doesn’t think that.
RENJUN: And it doesn’t matter if he does or not, right?
YERIM: (hiding the sadness that washes over her face, hesitantly) Right.
(The bell signals, and the four of them rise to their feet to go to their next class. As YERIM walks with JAEMIN, she makes eye contact with DONGHYUCK who’s sitting with his friends across the cafeteria. As soon as their eyes meet, JAEMIN pulls her out the door along with the others, who exit.)
ACT 2, SCENE 3
(The moonlight is peering through CHENLE’S bedroom window. It’s late and CHENLE is sitting at his desk in his dimly lit room, staring down stressfully at a pile of homework before him. He leans forward, picking up his pencil and concentrating, when his phone goes off beside him. Jumping from the sudden sound, he picks it up after growing confused at the caller ID.)
CHENLE: (sternly) What do you want, Donghyuck?
DONGHYUCK: Chenle, I’m sorry
CHENLE: (surprised for a second, but hurriedly erases it with carelessness) What? I don’t care how sorry you are, and either way, it’s not me you should be apologizing to.
DONGHYUCK: Yerim blocked my number (sighs) Just – please, Chenle, it’s important.
CHENLE: (cuts off DONGHYUCK) You like her, I know.
DONGHYUCK: (surprised) Huh, you knew?
CHENLE: (chuckling at his reaction) I could tell, but that doesn’t change what you did, even if you realize that your feelings are mutual now.
DONGHYUCK: (voice grows low and sentimental) But that’s the thing, they were always mutual.
CHENLE: (confused from the confession) What?
DONGHYUCK: I was scared to admit it to myself. I tried to make it seem like her smarts was all I wanted, but seeing her with that Jaemin kid, it makes me so angry.
CHENLE: (snorts humorously) Yeah, it’s called jealousy. (exhales) Sorry, Hyuck, I can get where you’re coming from, but you still broke my best friend’s heart, and she seems to of moved on now.
DONGHYUCK: (dully) Is she with Jaemin now?
CHENLE: No, but he seems to like her a lot.
DONGHYUCK: (reluctantly) Does she like him?
CHENLE: I don’t know, Jun and I have refrained from asking her in case she’s still shaken up from what you did.
DONGHYUCK: (feeling guilty, his voice grows soft) I’m sorry, Chenle, just please help me get her back, I’m begging you.
CHENLE: (dubiously sighs, hitting his pencil against the desk in thought for a moment) Donghyuck, I really don’t think –
DONGHYUCK: (cuts him off desperately) Please, I’ll do whatever it takes. I’ve never been more serious for a girl, believe me – as your old friend.
CHENLE: (groans out of frustration, feeling empathic) Alright, fine, I’ll help you, just because I still trust in you, as your old friend.
DONGHYUCK: (letting out a breath of relief, exclaims joyfully) Yes, thank you!
CHENLE: (strictly commanding) Just don’t mess this up or I’ll kill you.
DONGHYUCK: I won’t, I promise.
(CHENLE ends the call, letting out a deep exhale of air. His face is a clear sign of stress, and he runs his hand through his hair, suddenly not feeling the energy to do his homework anymore.)
CHENLE: (rising from his feet and walking over to his bed) I’ll just ask the others tomorrow. (He falls onto his mattress, and the lights go out.)
ACT 2, SCENE 4
(The next couple of days pass treacherously slow. All the whilst CHENLE had informed DONGHYUCK as much as he possibly could to help him get to YERIM, even telling him about the specific route she takes to purposely avoid him. Currently, she was walking down that hall now, earbuds in her ears and in her own world, not paying attention to anything else.)
DONGHYUCK: (standing firmly at the double doors she’s going to reach soon, a Starbucks cup in his hand. He calls out, waving his arm in the air.) Hey, Yerim!
(YERIM sees DONGHYUCK waving to her, causing her eyes to nearly bulge out of their sockets. Hurriedly, she spins to go the other direction.)
YERIM: (mumbling in disbelief) How’d he know I go this way?!
(Suddenly, CHENLE rounds the corner she was just about to turn down, and acts shocked when he sees her.)
CHENLE: Yer, where are you going? The cafeteria’s the other way.
YERIM: (dumbfounded, whisper yelling) Do you not see who’s standing at the other side of this hall?!
CHENLE: (looks over her shoulder, acting as if he hadn’t known DONGHYUCK would be there. They make eye contact, and DONGHYUCK frowns at him, his eyes pleading.) Donghyuck? Weird that he figured out you come this way; he must want to talk to you.
YERIM: (her eyes brighten for a split second, but she immediately shakes her head at the thought) No, he doesn’t. Why tell me that? I thought you agreed with Renjun.
CHENLE: (shrugging, his face showing obvious signs of sympathy) I don’t know, Yer, Hyuck’s my friend. I know what those texts said, but perhaps he realized he made a mistake.
YERIM: (pauses for a while, debating internally with herself, before deciding against it) I don’t care what he realized, it’s too late. (Sticking her nose in the air, she pushes passed CHENLE, going the opposite way of DONGHYUCK. YERIM exits.)

ACT 2, SCENE 5
(After lunch, YERIM had walked with JAEMIN to class. Everyone seemed more hectic than usual, much to her confusion. The teacher had announced they’d be going on a trip with assigned partners, causing her eyes to widen as she shifted in the front row which she sat at.)
JAEMIN: (sitting beside YERIM as he’d moved seats, leans over to whisper) What are the chances we’ll be partners?
YERIM: (chuckles doubtfully) Not very likely.
(YERIM’S name is called, and she sits up straight in her chair, mentally praying JAEMIN’S will be called next. However, she announces a name she hadn’t been expecting to hear, much to her dismay.)
YERIM: (gasping in incredulity) You’re kidding (her voice is a mumble as it sinks further into her seat) Tell me she didn’t just say Donghyuck.
JAEMIN: (feeling angry about such a selection, but quickly hides it as he frowns) I’m pretty sure that’s what I heard.
(A groan of emotional pain leaves her lips as she buries her face in her hands.)
JAEMIN: (rubbing her back comfortingly) It’s okay, Yerim. I’ll keep an eye on you in case anything happens.
YERIM: (still upset but feeling better from his words) Okay.
(At the back of the room, a big smile spread over DONGHYUCK’S lips. Once the bell rings only seconds later, he rises to exit the class, but stops by YERIM’S desk, causing both her and JAEMIN to whip their heads up to stare at him.)
DONGHYUCK: (giddily grins down at them, as if he had not one care in the world) See you at the trip, partner!
(DONGHYUCK exits.)
ACT 3, SCENE 1
(The day of the trip arrives. YERIM had avoided DONGHYUCK despite them being partners, having had sat with JAEMIN on the bus ride there instead. Once they arrived, he’d had to go with his own partner, leaving her to walk around the lonely, abandoned ghost town by herself. The wind blows through her hair as she walks, and she shivers from her lack of warm clothing.)
DONGHYUCK: (yelling through the wind, running as fast as his legs will take him to catch up with her) Yerim, hey Yerim!
YERIM: (confusion written on her face; she turns to see who was calling her. When she sees DONGHYUCK, her eyes bulge and she breaks into a run, calling behind her) Leave me alone, Donghyuck!
DONGHYUCK: (growing tired and panting from running) Wait, please, just hear me out!
YERIM: (continues running at full speed, yelling behind her) No, I don’t care to!
(YERIM rounds a corner in the old town, seemingly empty of any life forms, and heads towards an old building that looks trustworthy enough. DONGHYUCK is close behind her, and just as she runs inside, he was right behind her. She hadn’t noticed him enter until it was too late, and the wind slams the door shut behind the two of them. The lights go out.)
ACT 3, SCENE 2
(A gasp leaves YERIM’S lips, and she rushes over to the closed door, the old floorboards of the house creaking under her weight. Her hands pull at the handle, but it doesn’t budge.)
YERIM: (shaking in fear, turning away from the door and covering her face with her hands) Oh my God, we’re stuck in here! (she nervously looks at DONGHYUCK, exclaiming in even more worry) I’m stuck with you!
DONGHYUCK: (shaking his head at how he presumed she was overreacting and walking over to the door) I’m sure you’re just weak, chill out. (he grabs the handle himself, pulling at it with all his might, but it didn’t move an inch from its previous position.)
YERIM: (trembling with anxiety, she slides onto the floor against the wall, stuttering) W-What if no one finds us? What if we get stuck in here forever and they forget us? (burying her face in her knees that are pulled to her chest) I can’t believe this; we’re going to die in here!
(Gradually, DONGHYUCK sits beside YERIM. There’s a humorous expression on his face, but he tries hard not to laugh at how cute he found YERIM’S behavior.)
DONGHYUCK: (accidentally letting a small laugh escape) Yerim, you’re overreacting –
YERIM: (cutting him off, exasperatedly) Why are you laughing?! (heaves out a distressed sigh) Of course you’re laughing, you must really like seeing me upset.
DONGHYUCK: (frowning from her accusing words) No, that’s the last thing I ever want to see.
(YERIM only scoffs at his words, not believing them one bit. She crosses her arms over her chest, sitting up straight, and stares at the wall ahead of her.)
DONGHYUCK: (clearing his throat awkwardly) You know, I’m sorry, I’m sorry I hurt you. Just please stop running away from me.
YERIM: (still refusing to look at him, stubbornly, thinks back to how he’d been trying to bring her coffee the last few weeks) Bringing me coffee and waiting at the end of the hall I always go down isn’t going to change it.
DONGHYUCK: (states matter-of-factly) No, but I want to do something that will.
(YERIM turns to look at DONGHYUCK, feeling lost and wanting to see if he was actually serious. However, when she turns her head to peer up at him, he’s already gazing down at her, his face so close to hers their noses were almost touching.)
DONGHYUCK: (whispering) Are you cold? (He doesn’t wait for her to answer as he pulls his hoodie over his head, moving to slip it over YERIM’S. Out of shock and a certain feeling in her chest, she doesn’t object.)
YERIM: (mumbling nervously from how close their faces were, trying to regain her strong courage she seemed to have lost) Donghyuck…
(They stare at each other for a moment. His face only seemed to grow closer to hers.)
DONGHYUCK: (breaking the silence in a low tone) What is it, Yerim?
(Steadily, his face grows closer to hers, and he turns his head at an angle. DONGHYUCK’S eyes flicker from YERIM’S, to her lips.)
YERIM: (hesitant, her buried emotions making her a mess) I-I’m not sure…
DONGHYUCK: (his lips only centimeters away, his eyes only half open) If you don’t want me to, tell me to stop.
YERIM: (losing control of her wild emotions, she whispers back) Don’t.
(DONGHYUCK kisses YERIM, and she’s tense at first before she relaxes. Her arms go around his neck. Suddenly, the previously closed-door bursts open, and YERIM harshly shoves DONGHYUCK away, almost causing him to fall over. A look of shock is evident on her face from what happened as she turns to see a confused JAEMIN.)
JAEMIN: (confused, as he hadn’t saw what happened.) Are you okay, Yerim? We have to leave soon.
YERIM: (out of breath, her heart beating out of her chest) Yeah – I’m fine, Jaems.
JAEMIN: (still concerned, he walks over and grabs her hand) Come on, let’s go.
(JAEMIN drags YERIM to the door. She looks back at a sad, longing DONGHYUCK who only forced a smile. JAEMIN and YERIM exit.)
ACT 3, SCENE 3
(The next day, YERIM is walking her usual route to lunch. Like every other day, DONGHYUCK is standing at the end of the hallway. For the first time the last few weeks, she hesitates before walking towards him as he excitedly approaches her.)
YERIM: (pulling out her earbuds, nodding towards him as a greeting) Donghyuck.
DONGHYUCK: (stopping in front of her, holding out the coffee with a grin plastered on his face) Mocha Frappuccino?
YERIM: (raises her eyebrows, eyes widening in surprise, slowly she takes the coffee) My favorite, how’d you know?
DONGHYUCK: (shrugs, dismissing the subject) Actually, there’s something I wanted to talk to you about.
YERIM: (nervously purses her lips as she has a feeling what it’s about, she begins to walk towards the exit) Maybe later.
DONGHYUCK: (pouting) But Yerim –
YERIM: (cuts him off, sending him a small smile) Thanks for the coffee (already at the double doors that head to the cafeteria, but turns around to call) Just text me, I unblocked your number!
(YERIM exits.)
ACT 3, SCENE 4
(CHENLE and RENJUN are sitting at their normal table. CHENLE notices YERIM hadn’t come into the cafeteria with JAEMIN, and that DONGHYUCK was absent as well. A smirk spreads across his lips as he thinks about something going right for once, and RENJUN notices the look on his face.)
RENJUN: (narrowing his eyes at the mischievous looking boy, asking accusingly) What did you do?
CHENLE: (jumps a bit from his sudden voice, turning to him in confusion) What now, Renjun?

ACT 4, SCENE 1
(DONGHYUCK talking with CHENLE before history class. They’re standing at a proximity, talking in low voices in hope no one can hear their words.)
DONGHYUCK: (pursing his lips for a moment) I really want to ask her to prom, it seems like we’re close enough now, right?
CHENLE: (shrugging) I’d say so, it’s been a few weeks.
DONGHYUCK: (jumping out of excitement) Alright, I’ll ask Yerim after school!

RENJUN: (narrowing his eyes at DONGHYUCK, clearly having a dislike towards him) you’re asking my best friend what now?

ACT 4, SCENE 2
(YERIM is sitting at her seat, her chin in the palm of her hands as she stares at the ceiling. She felt abnormal, unsure as to why she didn’t feel as happy as she felt she should. JAEMIN, who’s sitting beside her, pokes her cheek and snaps her out of her deep thoughts.)
JAEMIN: (concerned) You okay?
YERIM: (offers a smile) Yup, just thinking about prom.
JAEMIN: (grins, reassuringly) Me too, I can’t wait!
YERIM: (halfheartedly returns his gesture) Me neither.

DONGHYUCK: Yerim, can you meet me outside in the garden after school?

DONGHYUCK: (grinning deviously) You’ll see, it’s important.

DONGHYUCK: Good!

YERIM: (mumbling) Wonder what that was about.
JAEMIN: (putting on an act as he hums) Who knows.

ACT 4, SCENE 3
(The sun beams down against YERIM as she walks into the school garden where DONGHYUCK had said they would meet. The flowers around her bloom what seemed like a million vibrant colors, and she was distracted by them until the sound of footsteps coming towards her was heard.)
YERIM: (looking up to see DONGHYUCK holding a bouquet of flowers, a gasp leaving her lips unknowingly) Donghyuck!
DONGHYUCK: (biting his lip to suppress his giggle at her reaction) I hope you don’t mind, I just made this ten minutes ago, but it’s for you. (he walks closer until he’s standing directly before her, holding out the flowers.)
YERIM: (mouth agape from shock, glancing from him to the flowers every few seconds. She mumbles as she takes the flowers) What is this?
DONGHYUCK: (chuckles, jokingly stating) They’re flowers, Yer.
YERIM: (giving him an annoyed look) No – I mean – why are you giving me flowers? (she tears her gaze from his as she realizes something, her voice growing that of a mouse) Is this about the kiss?
DONGHYUCK: (running a hand through his hair, anxiously laughs) No, Yerim, I wanted to ask you to prom.
YERIM: (coughs on air from astonishment, nearly dropping the flowers. She raises her voice unintentionally from shock) You what?!
DONGHYUCK: (chuckling from how cute she is) Prom – will you be my date to prom?
YERIM: (trying to process what’s happening, stumbling over words) I–I’m (as she thinks, her startled expression fades, and is replaced with one of despair) I-I can’t, I’m sorry, I’m sort of going with Jaemin.
DONGHYUCK: (coughs a bit, trying to hide how much her words had wounded his heart) Oh, oh okay, that’s fine! (he hurriedly backs away, turning to leave, his tone only that of a mumble) I’ll see you around, Yerim.
YERIM: (pouting as she feels her own heart ache in her chest, calling out in lost hope) Donghyuck, wait!
(DONGHYUCK only shakes his head, offering nothing but a short wave as he exits.)

CHENLE: (reassuringly) Just because she’s going with Jaemin doesn’t mean anything, she still likes you, Hyuck.
